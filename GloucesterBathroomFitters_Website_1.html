Our Services

Bathroom Installation Services

We offer a complete, end-to-end bathroom installation service for homeowners across Gloucester, Cheltenham and Stroud. Every project is managed by Tim personally — from the initial design consultation through to the final finish. No subcontractors, no surprises, just exceptional results.

What's Included in Every Installation

We handle everything, so you don't have to coordinate multiple trades.

01

Design Consultation

We start by understanding your home, your style and your vision. Whether you have a clear idea or need guidance, we'll help you make the right choices — from layout and fixtures to tiles and finishes.

02

Full Strip Out & Preparation

Every installation begins with a clean slate. We remove all existing fittings, prepare the walls and floors properly, and ensure everything is watertight before a single tile goes up.

03

Plumbing & First Fix

All pipework, waste and supply are handled to the highest standard. We work cleanly and efficiently, protecting your home throughout.

04

Tiling & Flooring

From large-format porcelain and contemporary glass tiles to natural limestone and traditional stone, our tiling is precise, beautifully finished and built to last.

05

Fixture & Fitting Installation

Baths, showers, basins, vanity units, WCs, heated towel rails — everything fitted with care and aligned perfectly. We work with all leading bathroom brands and can advise on suppliers.

06

Finishing & Snagging

Every project finishes with a thorough snagging check. We don't consider a job complete until every detail is right and you are completely happy.

Specialisms

Where we excel.

Freestanding Bath Installations

The centrepiece of any classic bathroom. We have extensive experience positioning and plumbing freestanding baths to make the most of your space.

Natural Stone & Limestone

Sympathetic to period and Cotswold properties, we work regularly with limestone, travertine and natural stone — materials that require skill and experience to install correctly.

En-Suite Installations

Maximising smaller spaces without compromising on quality. En-suites designed to feel considered and complete, not an afterthought.

Accessible & Walk-In Bathrooms

Thoughtfully designed bathrooms that don't sacrifice elegance for accessibility. Walk-in showers, low-threshold baths and grab rail installation handled sensitively.

What We Don't Do

We focus exclusively on complete bathroom installations and renovations. We don't take on small repair jobs, leak fixes or single appliance replacements — this keeps our diary focused on the projects we do best, and means our clients get our full attention.

About

Meet Tim Deane.

There's a real difference between a bathroom that's been fitted and one that's been crafted. At Gloucester Bathroom Fitters, every project gets the same attention — the same care on the final grout line as on the first fix.

Tim has been fitting bathrooms across Gloucester and Gloucestershire for 25 years. What started as a passion for getting things right — properly right — has grown into one of the area's most trusted bathroom installation businesses.

Having worked on everything from sleek contemporary apartments in Cheltenham to centuries-old Cotswold stone cottages in Stroud, Tim brings genuine versatility to every project.
